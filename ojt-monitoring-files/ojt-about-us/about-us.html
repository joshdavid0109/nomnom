About the Project

Our cutting-edge OJT monitoring web application is meticulously crafted to optimize and elevate the monitoring process for both students and administrators. Featuring a user-friendly interface coupled with robust functionality, the application enables seamless updates for direct supervisors and teams. It efficiently monitors diverse requirements, including MOA, Medical Certificate, Curriculum Vitae, and more. To enhance user experience, the system incorporates a checklist feature for effortless tracking, filtering, and emailing of necessary documents.

Vision

In line with our vision, the application aims to automate the submission of weekly reports and streamline the evaluation process. By doing so, we aspire to provide a smooth, hassle-free experience for all users, fostering efficiency and effectiveness in the monitoring and reporting procedures.
